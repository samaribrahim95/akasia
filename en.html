According to statistics from the National Center for the Financial Sector Development Program, approximately 45% of Saudi citizens do not have savings, while 80% do not have investment plans. To address these challenges and empower citizens financially, the Akasia app was developed as an effective tool to support users in creating sustainable financial plans and facilitating access to diverse investment opportunities, in line with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030, which aims to enhance financial literacy and achieve financial independence for individuals..

To address these challenges and financially empower citizens, the Akasia app was designed to support users by helping them to establish sustainable financial plans and facilitate the processes of saving and investing.
